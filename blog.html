Rename Born A Crime

I would rename the Born a crime - "Echoes of My Childhood".
I would say the book narrates Trevor's childhood and most of what happened during his childhood
has shaped him to who he is today. He also connects his personal memories to the larger historical
happenies of that time. For Trevor to Navigate through the apartheid he used humour as armour. He used
it to talk to people and fit in and even to get out of trouble. One of his career paths ends up being comedy.
where he uses humour to make a living.
