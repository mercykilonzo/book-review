"Abel wanted a traditional marriage with a traditional wife… but he never fell in love with subservient women… 'He only wants a woman who is free because his dream is to put her in a cage.'”

Traditionally, most women were expected to be submissive to their husbands and serve them. I would
say they were raised up to be kept by men. Likewise in morden relationships this is not the case.
In modern society the definition of gender roles seems to have changed. what was termed to be men jobs
and roles, women roles and jobs has turned to be an outdated notion.

Just as Particia defied the rules of tradition and made a different life for herself, most women
tend to do the same currently. They aspire to take roles which previously we designated only for men,
and even do them better than men. Though society still expects her to be under a man. My opinion would be:
Love should not be a way for a man to cage a woman but it should be honouring each other's freedom, even when the tradition dictates otherwise.
